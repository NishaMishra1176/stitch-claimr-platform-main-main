## Amazon Reconciliation Integration
Amazon reconciliation issues flow through the same stages:
- **Stage 0**: Identified as "Reconciliation Issue" in panic interceptor
- **Stage 1**: Classified as "Amazon Settlement Mismatch" in triage
- **Stage 2**: Forensic analysis of SAFE-T claims and reconciliation data
- **Stage 3**: Evidence of settlement discrepancies and timeline violations
- **Stage 4**: Escalation to Amazon seller support and legal channels
- **Stage 5**: Recovery tracking for Amazon settlements
- **Stage 6**: Pattern analysis of Amazon-specific issues

## Conversion Optimization Features

### Panic-First Approach
- Every page acknowledges user is in crisis mode
- Immediate problem identification rather than feature exploration
- Urgency and scarcity elements throughout

### Forced Decision Funnels
- Users must select their specific issue type
- No browsing mode allowed
- Clear progression indicators

### Evidence Preservation
- Automatic evidence capture and storage
- SLA countdown timers
- Risk visualization tools

### Legal Pressure
- Direct escalation to nodal officers
- Automated legal notice generation
- Court filing guidance

### Recovery Tracking
- Money recovery dashboards
- Success rate metrics
- Timeline tracking

## Instant Closure Paths
Every page includes direct paths to closure without requiring full funnel completion:
- "Escalate Now" button on every page
- "Generate Legal Notice" option
- "Contact Recovery Expert" option
- "Force Closure" option

## Clickability Rules
All elements are clickable with `<a href>`:
- Cards and tiles
- Stats and numbers
- Icons and symbols
- Section headers
- Warning banners
- Progress indicators

This ensures users can navigate freely between any stage of the recovery process while maintaining focus on the ultimate goal: money recovery.
