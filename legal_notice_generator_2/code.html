Legal Notice

Under Consumer Protection Act, 2019

Date: October 24, 2023
Regd Post AD / Speed Post

To,

The Managing Director / Legal Officer,

Delhivery Limited

Corporate Office, Gurgaon, Haryana.

Subject: NOTICE FOR DEFICIENCY IN SERVICE AND UNFAIR TRADE PRACTICE REGARDING FAKE RTO OF ORDER.

Dear Sir/Madam,

I, the undersigned, am a seller operating on e-commerce marketplaces. I am writing to you to formally raise a dispute regarding the shipment handled by your company with Order Value ₹12,500.

That despite the goods being available and the customer being reachable, your delivery personnel falsely marked the status as "Customer Not Available" / "RTO Initiated" without attempting valid delivery. This constitutes a clear "Deficiency of Service" under Section 2(11) of the Consumer Protection Act, 2019.

I hereby call upon you to compensate the loss of ₹12,500 along with interest @ 18% p.a. within 15 days of receipt of this notice, failing which I shall be constrained to initiate legal proceedings in the appropriate Consumer Dispute Redressal Commission.

Authorized Signatory
